Official Description:

Keymaster is a god that lives in The Void. His job, (or in His words, purpose) is to go around to different universes, and remove any anomalies he may find there. Anomalies, you know, things that aren't supposed to be there. He really enjoys what he does, or at least that's what he says.
Keymaster has a vast assortment of Abilities at his disposal. The one he likes to use the most is his Ability to conjure things. To focus the energy within him into a solid object of any material he wishes. His most common use for this is creating arms with it.
