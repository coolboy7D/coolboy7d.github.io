Official Description:

Pastel is a female book object who shares an apartment with Raven. The two have become great friends over time. Pastel sells digital art commissions online in order to pay her part of her and Raven's rent.
Pastel's Ability is that she can transform any stylus she is currently holding into a weapon. Which weapon it converts into depends on the stylus she's holding. Her own stylus, when converted into a weapon, results in a katana.
Pastel always tries her best to be nice to people, as she is aware this is how she makes good friends, like Raven. She will try her best to give you second chances, though she does know when enough is enough and will be sure to make sure you know that. Raven and Pastel happen to make an amazing duo together, particulary due to their shared living situation, and shared interests.
